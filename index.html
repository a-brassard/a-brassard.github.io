I love languages and enjoy programming.

This has led me to the field of natural language programming (NLP), where I can freely ask myself - what is meaning, exactly? After receiving my Master's degree at the University of Zagreb in Computer Science, I started working at the Tohoku NLP Lab, where I am doing my best on NLP-related research.


Lately, I've been preoccupied with trying to create a challenge for sentence-encoding systems that would reveal whether they contain all the elements of a sentence's meaning.

This isn't just about grammar or logic trees - I want to dig deeper.
