Research interest

I work in Natural Language Processing with a particular interest in commonsense reasoning. My PhD topic is thinking of ways to diagnose current systems' reasoning capabilities, beyond what a single performance score can tell us. Just getting started!


Currently, I am working on a structured explanation dataset for commonsense reasoning tests and comparing human-given explanations to existing knowledge bases.
